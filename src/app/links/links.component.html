Resources to Become a STEM Goddess

Courses

  • CodeAcademy includes courses for your student to learn how to code.

  • KhanAcademy is a free online service filled with courses on various programming languages to jumpstart your child's interest in STEM

  • Udemy is an excellent online resource with ample courses on programming and tech related skills

Organizations

  • EngineerGirl provides great insight regarding careers in engineering.

  • Citi Women in IT Internship Program provides an engaging and fast paced work experience for aspiring engineers and computer scientists.

  • Girls Who Code will allow your student to start their own club and enroll in seminars and workshops designed for women interested in STEM.
