What is STEM?

STEM is a commonly used acronym meant to encompass Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math. STEM professionals can be involved in all of its components or they can specialize in just one field. STEM professionals may include: engineers, doctors, physicists, chemists, astronauts, and more! The possibilities are endless, and we dare you to explore them.
